# No-Show Medical Appointments Analysis

**Brazil 2016 – 110,527 appointments**

Analysis to identify why patients miss their medical appointments and which factors matter most.

## Key Findings
- **Wait time** is the strongest predictor (>30 days → 30%+ no-show)
- Young adults (15-35) with long waits and no SMS reminder = highest risk (34%)
- SMS reminders help long-wait patients
- Scholarship patients show up less often
- Large differences between neighbourhoods
